What is a Scam?

A scam is when someone tries to trick you into giving away your money or personal information. Scammers might pretend to be from a trusted company, a bank, or even a family member.

They often pressure you to act quickly — for example, saying your account will be locked, or you've won a prize. Don’t panic. Think before you click or respond.

Never share your passwords, credit card numbers, or bank details with people who contact you unexpectedly.
