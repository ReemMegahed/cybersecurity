Password Safety

Strong passwords help protect your accounts from hackers. Here's how to keep your passwords safe:

  • 🔐 Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • 🔑 Avoid using personal information like your name or birthday.
  • 🔁 Don’t reuse the same password on different websites.
  • 📱 Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) when available.
  • 🧠 Use a password manager if you find it hard to remember passwords.

💡 Remember: a strong password is your first line of defence.
